Keeping you and your data safe.

We’re open by default, freely and easily accessible to all. OPEN Data covers general, not personal information. No individuals can be identified from our datasets and the portal is secure for everyone to use and benefit from. Public, private, academic and voluntary organisations are invited to open their data and use this platform to share it.
